The Preparation, Characterization and Electrochemical study of Ti2GaN nanocomposite

The aim of the project is to find a suitable compound with good electrical properties for a supercapacitor and then to synthesize it into a nanostructured state. The aim of the project is to design a MATLAB simulation layout for a solar panel installation at home, which can operate on a standalone basis and also can connect to the grid.
